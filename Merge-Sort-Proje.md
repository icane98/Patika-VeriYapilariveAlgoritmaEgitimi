## [16,21,11,8,12,22] -> Merge Sort

1. [16,21,11] - [8,12,22]
2. [16], [21,11] - [8], [12,22]
3. [16], [11,21] - [8], [12,22]
4. [11,16,21] - [8,12,22]
5. [8,11,12,16,21,22]

*  O(nlogn)
